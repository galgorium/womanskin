Reka Nyari is an audiovisual artist based in New York<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

His work ranges from fashion and fine art photography and videography, to the creation of installations and performances.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Her style, between eroticism and melancholy, explores the beauty and sexuality of bodies, from a predominantly female perspective.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The body art portrayed in his photographs tells a story, each line of ink is part of a narrative.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
